## ⚽ FIFA World Cup Corner

*Your fan card. Tell us who you ride with, what you know about the beautiful game, and what you expect from this year's tournament.*

#### My Nation & Why:

- I am representing Brazil in μFIFA because they bring an energy and artistry to football that no other nation matches. The way Brazil plays — jogo bonito, the beautiful game — feels closest to how I want to approach my work: creative, fluid, and always pushing forward.
- It is also legacy. Five World Cup titles, legends like Pelé and Ronaldinho, and a fanbase that bleeds yellow and green. Representing Brazil in μFIFA feels like carrying a proud tradition.

#### Supporting Team in the Real World Cup 2026:

- In the actual FIFA World Cup 2026, I will be backing Brazil all the way. With a young, hungry squad and something to prove after recent heartbreaks, I believe this could be their year to reclaim the trophy.
- My loyalties are consistent — both in μFIFA and on the real pitch, it is always Brazil.

#### All-Time Favourite Player:

- Ronaldinho is my all-time favourite footballer. The joy he played with, the audacious flicks, the bicycle kicks, and that smile — he made football look like art. He never just won games; he made people fall in love with the sport.
- His story also resonates with me: raw talent combined with relentless creativity, never afraid to try something unexpected.

#### Best Player Right Now:

- Vinicius Jr. is the best player in the world right now, and I will make that case confidently. His pace, dribbling, and composure in front of goal have reached an elite level. At Real Madrid he has consistently delivered in the biggest moments, and he carries Brazil's hopes as well.
- He is exciting to watch every single game, and that consistency at the highest level sets him apart.

#### Past World Cup Memories:

- The 2022 Qatar World Cup semifinal when Brazil were knocked out on penalties by Croatia is a memory that still stings. Neymar scored a stunning extra-time goal and the crowd was electric — then everything unravelled in the shootout.
- I watched it with family and the silence in the room after Marquinhos hit the post said everything. Heartbreak, but the kind only football can give you.

#### 2026 Predictions:

- Who wins: Brazil — I am going all in on this one. The squad is maturing at the right time and the motivation after 2022 is fierce.
- Dark horse: Morocco. They shocked the world in 2022 and they are only getting better. A deep run in 2026 is very possible.
- Player to define the tournament: Vinicius Jr. He has the talent, the stage, and the hunger to be the face of this World Cup.
- Match I am most looking forward to: Brazil vs Argentina. Whenever these two meet it is more than football — it is history, passion, and theatre all at once.

#### μFIFA World Cup 2026 - Tournament Goals:

- By the end of μFIFA, I want to have at least one project live and publicly accessible that I can point to as proof of work. Something built from scratch, shipped, and shareable.
- I also want to contribute meaningfully to open-source, engage with the μLearn community, and come out of this tournament with new collaborators, new skills, and a portfolio that speaks for itself.
